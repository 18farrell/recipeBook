Fried Chicken Recipe

Description

They dont call it the taste of the south for nothing. A good chicken fry, can make you cry. One has not truly lived until they have had Buttermilk Fried Chicken with green beans and mash

Ingredients

  • Chicken Drumsticks
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Flour
  • Seasonings
    • salt
    • pepper
    • cayenne
    •
  • Step 1: brine salted chicken in buttermilk
  • Step 2: leave overnight
  • Step 3: combine flour with seasoning until aromatic
  • Step 4: flour should be full of color and smell heavenly
  • Step 5: take chicken out of milk and put in flour
  • Step 6: repeat process until all chickens are covered
  • Step 7: deep fry in 350f peanut oil until internal temp is around 160f (will cook while out of oil)
  • Step 8: Enjoy :D
